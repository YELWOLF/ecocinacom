# Ecocina Cinematic Video Storyboard

## Concept Overview

This storyboard outlines the visual narrative for a premium cinematic 3D video for "Ecocina," a modern kitchen brand specializing in design and commercialization. The video aims to communicate innovation, craftsmanship, premium design, trust, and a modern lifestyle through a seamless transformation from an architectural sketch to a photorealistic luxury kitchen.

## Scene Breakdown and Image Prompts

### Scene 1: Opening - Architectural Sketch

**Description:** The video opens with a minimalist, hand-drawn architectural kitchen sketch appearing progressively on a clean white background. The aesthetic is that of a refined black pencil or technical architectural line drawing, emphasizing a clean blueprint style.

**Image Prompt:**
```
A minimalist, hand-drawn architectural kitchen sketch, black pencil lines on a clean white background. The drawing is progressively appearing, showcasing a contemporary kitchen layout with shelves, countertops, and a kitchen island. Clean blueprint aesthetic, technical architectural drawing style. Focus on precision and detail. High resolution, professional rendering.
```

### Scene 2: Transformation Sequence - Mid-point Evolution

**Description:** The sketch smoothly evolves into 3D geometry. This mid-point image captures the transition where elements like walls, cabinets, countertops, and the kitchen island are beginning to emerge, but still retain a translucent or wireframe quality, blending the sketch with nascent 3D forms. The transition should feel premium, fluid, and futuristic.

**Image Prompt:**
```
A seamless transition from a minimalist architectural kitchen sketch to emerging 3D geometry. The scene shows elements like walls, cabinets, countertops, and a kitchen island partially formed in 3D, with some areas still appearing as translucent wireframes or glowing outlines. The background is clean and bright. The overall feeling is premium, fluid, and futuristic. Cinematic lighting, high resolution.
```

### Scene 3: Final Reveal - Photorealistic Luxury Kitchen

**Description:** The kitchen becomes ultra-realistic and photorealistic, showcasing premium matte cabinetry, elegant wood textures, stone or marble countertops, integrated lighting, and high-end appliances. The camera moves slowly around the kitchen island with elegant depth of field, highlighting a minimalist luxury aesthetic and a warm, sophisticated ambiance. No people are present; the focus is entirely on the kitchen environment.

**Image Prompt:**
```
An ultra-realistic, photorealistic modern kitchen interior. Features premium matte dark grey cabinetry, elegant warm wood textures, white marble countertops, integrated LED strip lighting under cabinets and within shelves, and high-end black built-in appliances. A large kitchen island with a white marble top is central. Slow cinematic camera movement, smooth dolly shot around the island. Elegant depth of field. Warm, sophisticated ambiance with subtle lighting transitions. Scandinavian/Italian modern kitchen inspiration. Neutral color palette (matte black, warm wood, soft beige, stone textures). High-end architectural visualization quality. Clean, elegant composition. No people. 4K quality, 16:9 aspect ratio.
```
